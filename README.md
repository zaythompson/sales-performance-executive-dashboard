# Sales Performance Executive Dashboard

### Power BI Portfolio Project

---

## Executive Summary

This Power BI project delivers an executive reporting solution designed to provide business leaders with actionable insights into sales performance. The dashboard consolidates key performance indicators (KPIs), regional performance, customer segments, product category analysis, and sales trends into a single interactive reporting environment that supports strategic, data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often rely on multiple reports to evaluate sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional results. This fragmented reporting limits executive visibility, delays timely decision-making, and makes it difficult to identify performance trends across the business.

This dashboard centralizes these metrics into a single interactive reporting solution, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance more efficiently and identify opportunities for growth.

---

## Project Objectives

- Develop an executive sales performance dashboard.
- Centralize business KPIs into one reporting solution.
- Monitor sales, profit, orders, average order value, and profit margin.
- Evaluate regional and customer segment performance.
- Analyze product category trends.
- Enable interactive analysis through bookmark navigation and filtering.

## Key Insights

- Consolidated executive KPIs into a centralized reporting solution.
- Compared regional sales, profit, and order performance.
- Evaluated customer segment performance across the business.
- Analyzed product category trends to support strategic decision-making.
- Improved dashboard usability through interactive navigation and filtering.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Prioritize investment in high-performing regions to maximize revenue growth.
- Investigate underperforming regions to identify operational or sales improvement opportunities.
- Monitor customer segment performance to support targeted marketing strategies.
- Review product category performance to guide inventory planning.
- Incorporate executive KPI reviews into routine business performance meetings.
